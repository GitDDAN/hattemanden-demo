Concept 1: Classic Elegance

Combination Mark

A refined, solid top hat icon paired with a sophisticated serif wordmark. The hat features gradient depth, a detailed band with buckle, and generous proportions. Best suited for a brand that wants to feel established and premium.

Concept 2: Heritage Monogram

Lettermark

The letter "H" with a top hat integrated into the left stem — the hat becomes part of the letterform itself. Uses uppercase tracking for authority. The monogram works beautifully at small sizes and as a standalone mark inside a circle.

Concept 3: Danish Minimalism

Scandinavian

Ultra-clean approach inspired by Nordic design principles. The top hat is rendered as a single-stroke outline — light, airy, modern. Lowercase wordmark in Inter conveys approachability while the gold hat band adds the only touch of warmth. The most "website-native" option.

Concept 4: The Gentleman's Mark

Emblem

A heritage emblem approach — the top hat sits inside a shield/crest shape, flanked by "EST. KOLDING" and decorative lines. This is the most prestigious option, evoking old-world craftsmanship and Danish heritage. Works well for signage, packaging, and premium contexts.
